Lesson 1: Hangul Consonants

Learn the basic Korean consonants (ㄱ, ㄴ, ㄷ, etc.)

  • ㄱ - g/k
  • ㄴ - n
  • ㄷ - d/t
  • ㄹ - r/l
  • ㅁ - m
  • ㅂ - b/p
  • ㅅ - s
  • ㅇ - ng / silent
  • ㅈ - j
  • ㅊ - ch
  • ㅋ - k
  • ㅌ - t
  • ㅍ - p
  • ㅎ - h

Lesson 2: Hangul Vowels

Learn the basic Korean vowels (ㅏ, ㅑ, ㅓ, etc.)

  • ㅏ - a
  • ㅑ - ya
  • ㅓ - eo
  • ㅕ - yeo
  • ㅗ - o
  • ㅛ - yo
  • ㅜ - u
  • ㅠ - yu
  • ㅡ - eu
  • ㅣ - i

Lesson 3: Basic Greetings

Learn simple greetings in Korean

  • 안녕하세요 (annyeonghaseyo) - Hello
  • 안녕히 가세요 (annyeonghi gaseyo) - Goodbye (leaving)
  • 안녕히 계세요 (annyeonghi gyeseyo) - Goodbye (staying)
  • 감사합니다 (gamsahamnida) - Thank you
  • 죄송합니다 (joesonghamnida) - Sorry

Level 1: Beginner

Simple sentences, daily expressions, numbers, and basic grammar.

Lesson 1: Daily Expressions

Common phrases for everyday life.

  • 저는 학생이에요 (jeoneun haksaeng-ieyo) - I am a student
  • 이거 얼마예요? (igeo eolmayeyo?) - How much is this?
  • 화장실 어디예요? (hwajangsil eodi-yeyo?) - Where is the bathroom?
  • 좋아요 (joayo) - It's good / I like it

Lesson 2: Numbers 11-20

Learn Korean numbers 11-20.

  • 11 - 십일 (sip-il)
  • 12 - 십이 (sip-i)
  • 13 - 십삼 (sip-sam)
  • 14 - 십사 (sip-sa)
  • 15 - 십오 (sip-o)
  • 16 - 십육 (sip-yuk)
  • 17 - 십칠 (sip-chil)
  • 18 - 십팔 (sip-pal)
  • 19 - 십구 (sip-gu)
  • 20 - 이십 (i-sip)

Lesson 3: Basic Grammar

Subject-object-verb order and particles.

  • 저는 밥을 먹어요 (jeoneun bap-eul meogeoyo) - I eat rice
  • 책을 읽어요 (chaeg-eul ilg-eoyo) - I read a book
  • 학교에 가요 (hakgyoe gayo) - I go to school
  • Particle examples: 은/는 (topic), 이/가 (subject), 을/를 (object)

Level 2: Intermediate Beginner

Vocabulary expansion, grammar practice, and conversation skills.

Lesson 1: Common Verbs

Basic verbs for daily conversation.

  • 먹다 (meokda) - to eat
  • 가다 (gada) - to go
  • 오다 (oda) - to come
  • 보다 (boda) - to see/watch
  • 읽다 (ilkda) - to read

Lesson 2: Simple Sentences

Form sentences using vocabulary learned.

  • 저는 밥을 먹어요 - I eat rice
  • 학교에 가요 - I go to school
  • 영화를 봐요 - I watch a movie
